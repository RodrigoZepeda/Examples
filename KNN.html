Instructions

  1. Click the plot to generate a new point in red .
  2. Choose the metric in which the $k$ neighbors will be considered (either) euclidean norm or infinity norm. Around the point the area comprised by the norm will appear.
  3. Use the Classify button to categorize your point (the area will be coloured according to the category).
  4. To delete a point use the keyboard delete button.
  5. To change the number of initial points change the value in Sample .
  6. Use the Restart button to generate a new sample.

KNN Algorithm

The $KNN$ algorithm considers a given sample of points classified according to a category. For a new point, it classifies the point according to the category of the majority of its $k$ neighbors (in the example, if the majority are green KNN classifies them as green; if the majority are yellow it classifies them as yellow ; in case of a tie it does not classify and they appear black ).
